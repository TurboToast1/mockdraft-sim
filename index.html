            About this sim

            A browser-only simulator for the 2026 NFL Draft. No server, no build step. Your picks save automatically to localStorage.

            Data cutoff

            April 21, 2026. The real draft began April 23, 2026 in Pittsburgh. Live results may diverge from the seed data below — use Live Draft Mode to mark prospects as already taken.

            Sources used

            • Draft order (257 picks) — NBC Sports NY, Sharp Football Analysis (Apr 20-21, 2026).
            • Prospect rankings — Daniel Jeremiah Top 150 (NFL.com, Apr 20), consensus via NFL Mock Draft Database, Dane Brugler (The Athletic), PFF, Mel Kiper (ESPN), Bleacher Report.
            • Team needs — CBS Sports, Yahoo Sports team-needs trackers (Apr 18-20).
            • Trade chart — Jimmy Johnson (picks 1–224), extrapolated linearly to 0 for picks 225–257.

            Known caveats

            • Heights/weights past the top 30 are position-typical estimates (marked * in the UI).
            • AI GMs are stingier on non-QB moves; they'll sometimes surprise you.
            • Prospect list is ~220 names. If the pool runs dry in R7, remaining picks auto-fill as "UDFA-grade."
